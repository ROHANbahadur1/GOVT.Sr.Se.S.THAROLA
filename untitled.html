Welcome to GSSS Tharola

About Our School

Government Senior Secondary School Tharola is a beacon of quality education located in the serene hills of Himachal Pradesh. Our school focuses on holistic development of students with values, discipline, and academic excellence. Established to cater to the needs of rural students, GSSS Tharola continues to shape the future generation with dedication and pride.

Principal's Message

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” – Nelson Mandela

At GSSS Tharola, we believe in empowering students through knowledge and discipline. Our dedicated staff, modern facilities, and student-friendly environment help in nurturing well-rounded personalities. We invite you to join our family and experience the joy of learning.

Admissions

Admissions for the academic year 2025-26 are open for classes 6th to 12th (Arts Stream only). Admission will be based on merit and seat availability. Interested parents and students can visit the school office on working days from 10 AM to 2 PM. Admission starts from 15 March 2025 and the last date to apply is 10 May 2025.

Required Documents:

  • Transfer Certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Class Marksheet
  • Aadhar Card
  • 2 Passport-size Photographs

Academics

We offer curriculum under the Himachal Pradesh Board of School Education (HPBOSE) for Arts, Science, and Commerce streams. Our school has experienced faculty, well-equipped science labs, computer lab, and a library with over 2000 books.
